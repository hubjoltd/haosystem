Sure! Here's a more detailed breakdown for each feature to guide you through the development process:

### **Onboarding:**

1. **Onboarding Checklist**:

   * **Design checklist UI**: Create a user-friendly interface to display all required onboarding tasks.
   * **Integrate task tracking**: Implement a system to track progress on each task (e.g., incomplete, in progress, completed).
   * **Add user-specific tasks**: Tailor tasks based on employee roles, departments, or specific needs.
   * **Notify when tasks are completed**: Trigger automatic notifications to HR or the employee when tasks are marked as complete.

2. **Asset Assignment**:

   * **Inventory management**: Set up an inventory system to track all available assets (laptops, phones, etc.).
   * **Assign assets to employee profiles**: Link specific assets to the employee’s profile based on their role or department.
   * **Track asset distribution**: Monitor where assets are, who they are assigned to, and when they were issued.
   * **Notify employee & manager**: Notify the employee of asset assignments and managers when the assets are distributed.

3. **Document Upload & E-Sign**:

   * **Create upload interface**: Build a form or drag-and-drop area for employees to upload required documents.
   * **Implement e-signature integration**: Integrate a secure e-signature service (like DocuSign or Adobe Sign) for signing documents.
   * **Link documents to employee profiles**: Ensure that documents uploaded and signed are linked to the correct employee’s record.
   * **Store and track signed documents**: Store documents securely in the cloud and track which documents have been signed.

4. **Welcome Kit**:

   * **Prepare digital/physical welcome kit**: Depending on the company, create either a digital or physical package containing helpful resources, company swag, or access credentials.
   * **Assign kit to new hires**: Automatically assign the welcome kit based on the employee's start date and location.
   * **Notify employee**: Send the employee a notification that their welcome kit is ready.
   * **Track delivery status**: If physical, track the shipping process to ensure timely delivery.

5. **Training (Initial Orientation)**:

   * **Set up training modules**: Design onboarding training courses (e.g., company policies, software tools, safety guidelines).
   * **Assign modules based on role**: Automatically assign relevant training sessions according to the new hire’s job position or department.
   * **Track completion**: Use a Learning Management System (LMS) to track when each training module is completed.
   * **Notify on upcoming sessions**: Send automated reminders to employees about upcoming or overdue training.

6. **Buddy/Manager Assignment**:

   * **Pair new hire with a buddy/manager**: Use an algorithm or manual pairing to assign a mentor or manager to guide the new hire.
   * **Send intro email**: Automatically send a welcome email introducing the new hire to their buddy or manager.
   * **Track interaction milestones**: Use a system to track interactions between the new hire and their buddy or manager, like check-ins or meetings.
   * **Feedback collection**: After a certain period, gather feedback from both the new hire and the buddy/manager to assess the relationship.

7. **Completion Status Tracking**:

   * **Design status dashboard**: Create a visual dashboard where employees, HR, and managers can see the status of all onboarding tasks.
   * **Show completion progress of tasks**: Each task should show the progress and whether it's pending, in progress, or completed.
   * **Notify users of pending actions**: Send reminders or alerts to employees or managers when tasks are overdue or approaching deadlines.

### **Training Management:**

1. **Training Session Setup**:

   * **Design session creation form**: Build a form where HR or trainers can input session details (e.g., title, description, trainer, etc.).
   * **Set date, time, and trainers**: Allow the scheduling of the session with the date, time, and a list of trainers or facilitators.
   * **Assign employees to sessions**: Automatically assign employees to sessions based on roles, departments, or training needs.
   * **Set reminders**: Send out automatic reminders to participants and trainers before the training session starts.

2. **Training Calendars**:

   * **Create calendar view**: Design a calendar that shows all upcoming training sessions, with filters for different departments or employee roles.
   * **Sync with employee schedules**: Integrate the calendar with employee availability or corporate calendars to avoid scheduling conflicts.
   * **Allow session filtering**: Users should be able to filter training sessions by type, trainer, or department.
   * **Send event reminders**: Set up automated notifications to remind employees of upcoming sessions.

3. **Employee Training Attendance**:

   * **Implement attendance tracking system**: Use an automated system (QR code, in-app check-in, or facial recognition) to mark attendance when employees join a session.
   * **Mark attendance via check-in**: Allow employees to check-in digitally (through an app or web portal) to confirm their presence.
   * **Sync data with employee records**: Automatically update the training completion status in the employee’s profile or LMS.
   * **Allow manual attendance correction**: Provide a way for trainers or admins to adjust attendance in case of errors.

4. **Training Feedback**:

   * **Create feedback survey form**: Design a post-training survey that gathers feedback on the session (e.g., usefulness, trainer performance, etc.).
   * **Send post-training surveys**: Automatically send surveys to participants after the training session ends.
   * **Analyze results**: Use data analytics to process feedback and identify areas for improvement in future training sessions.
   * **Share insights**: Provide trainers or HR with summaries of the feedback for continuous improvement.

5. **Training Certificates**:

   * **Design certificate templates**: Create customizable certificate templates with space for employee name, training date, session title, and signature.
   * **Auto-generate certificates on completion**: Once a participant finishes the training, automatically generate a certificate of completion.
   * **Email certificates to employees**: Send the generated certificate to the employee’s email address for their records.
   * **Store for reference**: Save certificates in the employee’s digital profile or database for future reference.
