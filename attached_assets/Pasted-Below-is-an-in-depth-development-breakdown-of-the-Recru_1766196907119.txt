Below is an **in-depth development breakdown** of the **Recruitment & Onboarding module (5.1 Recruitment)**, written as if you are designing or documenting an **HRMS / ATS system**. This covers **process flow, system features, data handling, approvals, and automation**.

---

## 5.1 RECRUITMENT – IN-DEPTH DEVELOPMENT

### 1. Manpower Requisition (MRF – Manpower Request Form)

**Purpose:**
To formally request approval for hiring based on business needs.

**System Development Details:**

* Digital MRF form initiated by **Hiring Manager**
* Fields include:

  * Department
  * Job Title & Role
  * Number of Positions
  * Employment Type (Permanent/Contract/Intern)
  * Location (Onsite/Remote/Hybrid)
  * Budgeted Salary Range
  * Justification (New Role / Replacement)
  * Expected Joining Date
* **Approval Workflow:**

  * Hiring Manager → Department Head → HR → Finance → Management
* Status Tracking:

  * Draft | Submitted | Approved | Rejected | On Hold
* Once approved, system **auto-creates a Job ID** for posting

**Automation:**

* Email/portal notifications for approvals
* Auto-lock editing after approval

---

### 2. Job Postings (Internal & External)

**Internal Posting:**

* Visible to existing employees via employee portal
* Supports internal mobility and promotions
* Option to restrict by department or grade

**External Posting:**

* Publish jobs to:

  * Company Career Page
  * Job portals (via API integration)
  * Social media (LinkedIn, etc.)
* Job description pulled directly from approved MRF

**System Features:**

* Job status: Open | Closed | On Hold
* Application deadline control
* Auto-hide expired jobs
* Equal Opportunity compliance tagging

---

### 3. ATS – Candidate Database

**Core ATS Structure:**

* Centralized candidate repository
* Each candidate has a **unique candidate ID**

**Candidate Profile Includes:**

* Personal details
* Resume & attachments
* Skills & experience
* Job(s) applied for
* Application source
* Current recruitment stage
* Interview history
* Notes & ratings

**Advanced Features:**

* Duplicate detection
* Candidate tagging (e.g., “High Potential”, “Future Hire”)
* GDPR/consent management
* Search & filters (skills, experience, location)

---

### 4. Resume Parsing

**Functionality:**

* Automatically extracts data from resumes (PDF, DOC, DOCX)

**Parsed Fields:**

* Name, Email, Phone
* Education
* Employment history
* Skills
* Certifications

**System Logic:**

* Maps parsed data to ATS fields
* Allows HR to manually edit parsed data
* AI-based skill matching with job requirements

**Benefits:**

* Reduces manual data entry
* Improves screening speed
* Enables smart shortlisting

---

### 5. Interview Scheduling & Feedback

**Scheduling Module:**

* Integrated calendar (Google/Outlook)
* Panel interview support
* Time-slot selection
* Automatic interview invites (Email + Portal)

**Interview Types Supported:**

* Telephonic
* Video
* In-person
* Technical / HR / Managerial rounds

**Feedback System:**

* Structured feedback forms per role
* Rating scale & comments
* Mandatory submission before next stage

**Automation:**

* Reminders to interviewers
* Status auto-update after feedback submission

---

### 6. Candidate Evaluation & Approval

**Evaluation Criteria:**

* Skills match score
* Interview ratings
* Cultural fit
* Salary expectations
* Background verification (if applicable)

**Approval Workflow:**

* Recruiter recommendation
* Hiring Manager approval
* HR validation
* Final management sign-off (optional)

**Decision Outcomes:**

* Selected
* On Hold
* Rejected
* Talent Pool

**Audit Trail:**

* Records who approved/rejected and when
* Ensures compliance and transparency

---

### 7. Conversion of Candidate → Employee

**Trigger:**
Candidate marked as “Selected”

**System Actions:**

* Auto-create Employee Master record
* Carry forward:

  * Personal details
  * Role & department
  * Salary structure
  * Reporting manager
* Generate Employee ID
* Assign login credentials

**Integration:**

* Payroll
* Attendance
* IT asset request
* Access provisioning

---

### 8. Offer Letters & Appointment Letters

**Offer Letter Generation:**

* Template-based (role-wise)
* Auto-populated fields:

  * Name
  * Designation
  * Compensation
  * Joining date
  * Terms & conditions
* Digital approval & e-signature support

**Appointment Letter:**

* Issued post-acceptance
* Stored in employee document repository

**Status Tracking:**

* Offer Sent | Accepted | Declined | Expired

---

### 9. Experience / Warning / Other HR Letters (Auto-Generation)

**Types of Letters:**

* Experience Letter
* Confirmation Letter
* Warning Letter
* Promotion Letter
* Relieving Letter
* Salary Revision Letter

**System Features:**

* Template management
* Auto-fetch employee data
* Approval workflow
* PDF generation
* Digital signature
* Secure employee access/download

**Compliance:**

* Letter version control
* Audit logs
* Date/time stamping

---

### Overall System Benefits

* End-to-end recruitment automation
* Reduced hiring cycle time
* Centralized data & reporting
* Compliance-ready workflows
* Seamless onboarding experience
